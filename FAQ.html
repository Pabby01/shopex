General Questions

What is ShopEx?

ShopEx is an e-commerce platform where you can find high-quality products at competitive prices. We strive to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for all our customers.

How can I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page, fill in your details, and follow the instructions. It only takes a few minutes to get started.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment gateways. You can view all available options at checkout.

Orders and Shipping

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the courier's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of purchase. The product must be in its original condition. For more details, visit our Returns and Refunds page.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected. Standard shipping usually takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days.
