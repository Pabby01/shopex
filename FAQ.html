Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the return policy?

You can return any product within 30 days of purchase as long as it is in its original condition. Please keep the receipt for a smooth return process.

2. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with the tracking number. You can use this number on our website under the 'Track Order' section.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can also use store credit or gift cards.

4. How do I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team by calling our helpline at +2349020250260 or emailing us at support@shopex.com. We are available 24/7 to assist you.

5. Can I change my order after placing it?

Yes, you can change your order within 2 hours of placing it. After this period, we begin processing the order and may not be able to accommodate changes.
